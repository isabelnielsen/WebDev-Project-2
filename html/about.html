About Me

A Look Into My Life

I grew up in Arizona in a large family of six siblings, three boys and three girls. My family has a diverse background. My father is from the United States and grew up in California, and my mother is an immigrant from the Dominican republic. I loved growing up with a large family, especially having a new title of being an aunt!

My interests include cooking, dancing, painting, and traveling. I danced since I was three years old and dancing was a big part of my life as an extracurricular activity. Yearly recitals were an important part of my life and my family would always cheer me on. To express my creativity I also enjoy cooking and painting. My family and I also love traveling, and try to go somewhere new every year!

My dream career would be to own an interior design company and help people create environments in which they feel comfortable, yet inspired. I would like to combine my business knowledge and my love for design to build a career that I will enjoy.
